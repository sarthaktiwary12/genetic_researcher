Their defining feature is the conserved J-domain, which mediates interaction with Hsp70 chaperones. They are crucial for maintaining protein homeostasis (proteostasis) under both normal physiological conditions and various stress conditions (e.g., heat, cold, drought, salinity, oxidative stress). In plants, they are found in various cellular compartments (cytosol, ER, mitochondria, chloroplasts, nucleus) and are involved in a wide array of cellular processes.
