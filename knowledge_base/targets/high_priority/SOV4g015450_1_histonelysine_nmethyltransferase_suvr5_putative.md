### 1. FUNCTION

**Known Facts (from *Arabidopsis* homologs):**
*   SOV4g015450.1 is annotated as a "Histone-lysine N-methyltransferase SUVR5 (putative)". The SUVR (Suppressor of variegation, Enhancer of zeste, Trithorax related) family proteins are part of the SET domain group (SDG) of histone methyltransferases.

*   SUVR proteins often function in maintaining gene silencing, particularly in response to stress and during development. For instance, *Arabidopsis* SUVR4, SUVR5, and SUVR6 have been implicated in drought stress responses.
