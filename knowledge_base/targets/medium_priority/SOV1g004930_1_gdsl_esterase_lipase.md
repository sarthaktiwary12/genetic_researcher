*   **Lipid metabolism:** Hydrolyzing various lipid substrates (e.g., acylglycerols, phospholipids, galactolipids) for energy mobilization, membrane remodeling, or signaling molecule production.

*   **Developmental processes:** Roles in embryogenesis, seed development, pollen development, and organogenesis.
*   **Hormone signaling:** Some GELPs are implicated in the metabolism or perception of plant hormones like abscisic acid (ABA), gibberellins (GA), jasmonates (JA), and salicylic acid (SA).
*   **Stress responses and defense:** Many GELPs are induced by biotic (pathogens, beneficial microbes) and abiotic stresses and participate in defense signaling, production of antimicrobial compounds, or programmed cell death. They can act as pattern recognition receptors or modify PAMPs.
