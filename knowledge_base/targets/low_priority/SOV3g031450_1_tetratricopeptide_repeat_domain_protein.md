*   Proteins containing TPR domains are involved in diverse cellular processes, including:
    *   **Chaperone activity**: Many TPR proteins act as co-chaperones, interacting with HSP70 and HSP90 to assist in protein folding, assembly, and transport (e.g., HOP/STI1, FKBP52).

    *   **Transcription regulation**: Some TPR proteins interact with transcription factors or chromatin remodeling complexes.
    *   **Stress responses**: Many TPR proteins are induced by various abiotic and biotic stresses and play roles in stress tolerance.
